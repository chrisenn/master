About Me

I am a UX Researcher who is passionate about user centred design. I really enjoy how design can be used to solve real problems and build empathy, particularly in a business environment. During my programming major at university, I studied an intro to HCI and I promptly fell in love with all things UX. I now have two years of experience under my belt as a UX practitioner. Much of that was as the sole UXer at my company, which gave me the opportunity to learn a lot in a short space of time. I got to sink my teeth into pretty much all aspects of UX including:
  • Generative research: surveys, interviews, affinity mapping, personas, experience maps, etc.
  • Evaluative research: usability testing, heuristic evaluation, tree testing, etc.
  • Prototyping: sketching, Axure, Photoshop, conducting ideation workshops/design studios, etc.
