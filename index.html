About Me

I am a UX Researcher who is passionate about user centred design. I have been a UX Researcher for two years. Having operated as a team of one, my experience is broad and covers:
  • Generative research: surveys, interviews, affinity mapping, personas, experience maps, etc.
  • Evaluative research: usability testing, heuristic evaluation, tree testing, etc.
  • Prototyping: sketching, Axure, Photoshop, conducting ideation workshops/design studios, etc.

Design Philosophy


Know Your User

We are not the users. Design decisions should be made based on research with real users.

Fail Fast

It's best to know about issues as early as possible. "Bad" news is good news if it saves you resources.

Substance Over Style

Aesthetics are important, but should never come at the expense of usability.
